Tutorial:
                Use the keyboard arrows or the right buttons menu to move the circle around.
                Made with only Vanilla JS by Saurav  (sauravabc16).

            Logic:
                *) If the ball is too close to the border it will change its color to Red.
                *) If the ball is too close to the border it will automatically stick to it.
                *) If the ball is too far from the border it will speed up.
